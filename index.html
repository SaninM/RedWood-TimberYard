Our Heritage

Three Generations of Timber Excellence

Since 1985, Redwood Timberyard has been the trusted source for premium timber products. Our family-owned business has grown from a small local yard to a regional leader, serving architects, builders, and craftsmen who demand the finest materials.

We meticulously source our timber from responsibly managed forests worldwide, ensuring both exceptional quality and environmental stewardship. Each piece is carefully selected and prepared by our master craftsmen to meet exacting standards.
